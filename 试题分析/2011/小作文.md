Suppose your cousin Li Ming has just been admitted to a university. Write him/her a letter to

1) congratulate him/her, and   
2) give him/her suggestions on how to get prepared for university life.

You should write about 100 words on ANSWER SHEET 2.

Do not sign your own name at the end of the letter. Use "Zhang Wei" instead.

Do not write the address. (10 points)

# 写作思路
## 背景：打散+改写

### 背景翻译 ：
假设你的堂弟 Li Ming 被大学录取，给他写一封信。
### 打散 +改写
假设你的堂弟 Li Ming **被你所在的** $\times \times$ 大学录取，给他写一封信。

## 主体

1. 积极参加校园活动（结交朋友、培养兴趣）
2. 利用自由时间多阅读（积累知识）
## 结尾

表达美好祝愿

### 分析
1. 称呼格式: 使用"<font color="#00b0f0">Dear Li Ming,</font>"，注意用逗号而非<mark style="background:#ff4d4f">冒号</mark>，给亲友写信不需要过于正式
2. I am 较为正式，给堂弟写信，可以使用 I‘m
3. <font color="#00b0f0">very</font> 的同义词替换 quite/particularly/especially
4. <font color="#00b0f0">happy</font> 的同义词替换 pleased/delighted/glad/excited
5. <font color="#00b0f0">have been admitted</font> 注意<mark style="background:#ff4d4f">不能</mark>使用 had been admitted 表示曾经被录取，有曾经被录取，现在被拒绝的意味。

- “charalates” → “features/characteristics”
- “give you change” → “give you a chance”
- “you speciality” → “your strengths/your specialty”
- “More over” → “Moreover”
- “compere” → “compared” / “compared with”
- “senior high school” 语法上需搭配 “compared with”
- “the study is important” → “study is important”

### 分析

特征在于：be characterized by/marked by/featured with
丰富的：a variety of/various/diverse/all kinds of/colorful
使用定语从句可避免重复表达，which can bring you opportunities to show your talent
